How We Started

Zoomies Cat Café began as a simple act of kindness—offering food, shelter, and love to stray cats in our neighborhood. What started as a small rescue effort soon blossomed into a cozy haven where both cats and humans could find comfort, companionship, and a second chance. Every purr, every cuddle, and every adoption tells a piece of our story.

  • Rescue Roots – We began by rescuing and caring for local strays in need.
  • Safe Sanctuary – Zoomies became a safe space for cats to heal and thrive.
  • Feline Family – Our early rescues became the heart of our community café.
  • Purposeful Sips – Every visit helps support care, rescue, and adoption efforts.
